Chana Masala

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 15 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 13.5 oz coconut milk
  • 2 (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • (Optional) cilantro

Steps

  1. Oil on medium low, medium size pot
  2. Stir in onion and garlic until translucent ~15 min (add a bit of water if onions get dry)
  3. Increase heat to medium, add curry and cumin, toast for 1 min
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, coconut milk and chickpeas
  5. Stir, particularly the bottom of the pot
  6. Reduce to low heat
  7. Simmer under thickened to liking ~10 min
