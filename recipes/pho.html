Pho Ga, serving size: 4 female adults/3 male adults

Ingredients

  • 1 rotisserie chicken (shredded)
  • 1 large onion / 2 small onions (halved)
  • 2 inches of ginger (sliced)
  • 1 qt (4 cups) of water
  • 2 or 3 star anise pods
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 4 tsp fish sauce
  • 3 tsp sugar
  • rice noodles
  • cilantro

Steps

  1. Oil on high heat, medium size pot
  2. Roast ginger slices and onion halves until black
  3. Pour water, chicken bones and skin, and all sauce ingredients
  4. When it boils, lower heat and simmer for 1.5 hours
